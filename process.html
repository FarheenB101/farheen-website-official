Process Overview

This page highlights the design and development phases of this website, from initial planning to final execution. Each step was undertaken with careful consideration of usability, aesthetics, and accessibility.



References

  • Design inspirations: W3Schools Forms
  • Accessibility guidelines: WCAG 2.0
  • Icons: Font Awesome
  • External YouTube video: Beabadoobee - Gallery
  • Google Maps embed: UTM Location
  • All gallery photo and video links are external, but the media is my original work.



Planning

  • Home Page (index.html): Introduces the website and links to each section.
  • Resume: Covers academic/work achievements, includes profile, skills, and poster.
  • Gallery: Shows hobbies and interests (drawings, book, clubs, cat photos, videos). Accessible formats included.
  • Contact: Links to LinkedIn, Instagram, UTM, interactive form, and map.
  • Process: This page! Includes design reflections and planning docs.


Design Thinking

I began with a sitemap focused on accessibility (WCAG 2.0) and a professional tone. My goal was to create a five-page site showcasing who I am academically, creatively, and professionally. I used a global navigation bar to make each page easily accessible.
